## Why ChurchCore

Most church management software was designed to handle contact lists and giving records. ChurchCore was designed to help you run a church responsibly — with the accountability your board expects, the child safety documentation your insurance requires, and the financial records your treasurer needs, all connected in one system.

Three things set ChurchCore apart from every other platform in this comparison:

**Compliance-first operations — child safety, audit trails, and tenant isolation built in.**
Your data never touches another church's system. Every church runs in a fully isolated environment, so there is no path for one congregation's records to appear in another's account. Children's ministry carries dedicated child safety controls: custody restrictions, authorized pickup enforcement, two-adult room coverage, incident logging, and session-specific check-in that only opens when a staff member enables it for that day. Every sensitive action — check-in, checkout, giving record, role assignment, data deletion — carries an audit trail that answers the question "who did this, and when?" even if a board member or insurer asks a year later.

**Finance integrity built in — real general ledger, fund mapping, and journal posting.**
ChurchCore is the only platform in this comparison with a full general ledger for churches. That means giving records map to specific funds, funds post to GL accounts, and journals reconcile automatically rather than living in a separate spreadsheet your treasurer maintains by hand. Donation receipts, fund-level reporting, and budget tracking are all part of the same connected record — not separate exports you paste together at year-end. No platform fees are taken from donations; every dollar given to your church arrives in your account.

**One connected system — people, events, giving, volunteers, communications, and member access all in one place.**
Rather than paying for four tools that don't talk to each other, ChurchCore connects your people directory, event calendar, giving history, volunteer schedule, children's check-in roster, and member communications into a single weekly readiness picture. Your administrator opens one screen to see what is ready, what needs attention, and what action to take — without switching systems.

## Who ChurchCore Is Built For

ChurchCore is designed for churches with **100 to 1,000 average attendance** that feel pressure in one or more of these areas:

- **Child safety accountability.** Your insurance carrier, your denomination, or your own leadership team needs documentation that your children's ministry follows safe-church policies — room ratios, custody restrictions, authorized pickups, and incident records.
- **Financial accountability to the board.** Your treasurer and board want giving records that tie to named funds, funds that tie to budget categories, and journals that close cleanly at year-end — not a giving-software export that someone re-enters into QuickBooks.
- **Fragmented systems.** You are currently using one tool for giving, a different one for check-in, a spreadsheet for volunteers, and email for communications. Staff time is spent re-entering data and chasing missing records between systems.
- **Board-level audit requirements.** Your elders, deacons, or governing board want to know that sensitive pastoral data, giving records, and personnel decisions are role-restricted and auditable — especially after a personnel change or insurance review.

ChurchCore is not yet the right fit for very large congregations (1,000-plus average attendance) that need deep reporting analytics, a native smartphone app for every member, or a broad integrations marketplace connecting dozens of third-party tools.

## Honest "Not Yet Competitive" Callouts

We want evaluators to make an informed decision. Here is where ChurchCore is not yet at the level of the mature platforms:

**Native iOS and Android apps.** ChurchCore members access their account through a mobile web experience that works well in a phone browser, but there is no native app in the Apple App Store or Google Play Store today. Planning Center Church Center, the Tithely app, and ChurchTrac Church Connect are ahead here.

**Integrations marketplace.** ChurchCore connects to Stripe for payments, Resend for email, and Twilio for SMS. It does not yet offer a broader marketplace of third-party integrations (accounting software sync, website embed plugins, background check services, or streaming platforms).

**Deep reporting and analytics.** Basic reports for giving, attendance, people, and events are in place. Decision-ready drill-downs, custom report builders, and multi-year trend dashboards are still maturing.

## Next Steps for Evaluators

**Read the application guide.** The guide at `docs/application-guide.md` walks through the weekly administrator workflow from setup through giving reconciliation.

**Request a pilot.** Contact the ChurchCore team to request a pilot account with your church's data structure. Pilot churches receive hands-on setup support and direct access to the development team for feedback.

**Questions about data migration.** If you are moving from Planning Center, Breeze/Tithely, or ChurchTrac, ask about the import wizard. It supports a dry-run preview so you can see exactly what will transfer before committing any records.
